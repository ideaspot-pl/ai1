warp
A speed unit invented by Gene Rodenberry for the explanation of extra-speed travel in Star Trek series. The speed of warp one is equal to the light speed. After warp one, the actual speed increases non-linearly.
Klingon
A species in Star Trek series characterised by violence and great fighting skills.
James T. Kirk
The captain of USS Enterprise in the original series of Star Trek. Starred by William Shatner.
